Club Objectives

To offer professional coaching for all skill levels.

The Badminton Club is committed to providing tailored coaching sessions catering to individuals at every skill level. From beginners seeking fundamental techniques to advanced players aiming for strategic refinement, our professional coaches curate personalized training to enhance skills and nurture a passion for the sport. Through structured guidance and personalized attention, we aim to empower members with the tools necessary to excel in badminton.

To organize regular training sessions and friendly matches.

We believe consistent practice is the cornerstone of improvement. The Badminton Club hosts regular training sessions, fostering an environment conducive to skill development, fitness enhancement, and team bonding. Additionally, friendly matches within the club serve as platforms to implement learned strategies and tactics in a supportive and encouraging setting. These sessions not only hone skills but also promote camaraderie among members, fostering a vibrant badminton community.

To participate in intercollegiate tournaments representing BMU.

As representatives of BMU, the Badminton Club is dedicated to showcasing the culmination of our hard work and dedication on the competitive stage. Participation in intercollegiate tournaments allows us to demonstrate our skills, sportsmanship, and teamwork. It's an opportunity not only to compete but also to learn, grow, and forge connections within the broader badminton community, proudly representing our institution with integrity and passion.

About Us

The BMU Badminton Club was established in 2014 by a group of badminton enthusiasts at BML Munjal University. Our mission is to promote the love for badminton, provide a platform for skill development, and foster a spirit of sportsmanship among students and staff.

Training Programs

Become a part of our dynamic community! Membership is open to all BMU students and faculty members. Our training programs cater to beginners, intermediates, and advanced players, ensuring personalized coaching to enhance skills.

Events and Tournaments

Stay tuned for our upcoming events! We host regular tournaments, intra-club matches, and workshops conducted by renowned badminton experts. Check out our calendar for exciting opportunities to showcase your badminton prowess.
